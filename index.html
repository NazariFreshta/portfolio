About Me

I am a passionate and determined learner from Afghanistan with a deep interest in leadership, communication, and social impact. Growing up in an environment where access to education—especially for women—remains a challenge, I have learned to see knowledge as both a privilege and a form of empowerment. Over the past few years, I have completed numerous online courses in leadership, communication, and even finance, which have strengthened my ability to guide others and think critically about real-world challenges. Beyond academics, I have volunteered as an English teacher for Afghan girls, an experience that not only deepened my sense of responsibility but also showed me the transformative power of education. I also created an English-language podcast centered on empowerment and storytelling, encouraging young Afghan women to stay hopeful and resilient. My long-term goal is to pursue a degree that equips me to lead meaningful change—by combining curiosity, compassion, and strategy to create opportunities where they are most needed.
